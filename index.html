    About us
    The mission & vision of RCCG:
    1) To make heaven.
    2) To take as many people with us.
    3) To have a member of RCCG in every family of all nations.
    4) To accomplish No. 1 above, holiness will be our lifestyle.
    5) To accomplish No. 2 and 3 above, we will plant churches within five minutes walking distance in every city and town of developing countries and within five minutes driving distance in every city and town of developed countries.
    6) We will pursue these objectives until every Nation in the world is reached for the Lord Jesus Christ.

OUR SERVICES

“...In Your presence, is fullness of joy.” - Ps. 16:11 (NKJV)

Join us every sunday at 8a.m to experience God afresh.

“Enter into His gates with thanksgiving, and into His court with praise. Be thankful to Him, and bless His name.” - Ps. 100:4 (NKJV)

Every first sunday of the month, is our thanksgiving service at 8a.m.

“Be diligent to present yourself approve to God, a worker who does not need to be ashamed, rightly dividing the word of truth." - 2 Timothy 2: 15 (NKJV)

Tuesday at 6:30p.m, is our digging deep, when we have an interactive session on the study of the scriptures.

“It shall come to pass that before they call, I will answer; And while they are yet speaking, I will hear.” - Isaiah 65:24 (NKJV)

Join us every Thursday at 6:30p.m for our prayer meeting tagged Faith Clinic, as we encounter the prayer-answering God.

“For as the heavens are high above the earth, so great is His mercy toward those who fear Him.” - Ps. 103:11 (NKJV)

Our 4-day monthly programme comes up between Wednesday preceding the last Saturday and last Saturday of the month at 6p.m daily and 7:00a.m on Saturday.

“Not forsaking the assembling of ourselves together...” - Heb. 10:25 (NKJV)

First Friday of the month, is Holy Ghost Service at Redemption Camp Km 46, Lagos-Ibadan Expressway at 6p.m.
